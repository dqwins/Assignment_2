Benefits of Sunshine

Nourish your soul with sunshine. Here are a few of the benefits of daily sunshine!

Lethbridge Sunlight Hours - A Year in Review

In 2022, here in Lethbridge, our average amount of days we received sunlight was 330 out of 365. That means that 90% of our days contained some amount of sunlight. It was a total of 2507 hours of sunlight. Here is a diagram of Alberta's total annual sunshine measured in kWh/kW/yr in 2022 from CurrentResults. You can see that Lethbridge is in one of the areas that receives the most amount of sunlight in all of Alberta.

I'd say we are pretty lucky to live here! So let's make the best of it and go out and enjoy some sunshine.
